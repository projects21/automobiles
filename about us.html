ABOUT US


Our History

Auto World was located in Sharjah, UAE. The company started its journey back in the early 1980s with the introduction of its 1984 Corvette model assembly kit. The company was started by the late Andrew Benjimen along with James Cain.

Auto World would later manufacture an estimated 8 million models per year; it would become a very productive company for Mr.Benjimen and a great company for the American and European hobbyists which many collectors had really enjoyed.

Back then, the model car business was a growing industry that generated great sales and revenue.The first promotional model car was manufactured in 1987. And although this store made others toy model kits, James George, Auto World's advertising manager at the time, once said, “We’re pretty much like a miniature car company.”

In conclusion, The Auto World Corporation, is one of largest automotive miniature companies in the world.
